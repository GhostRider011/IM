Shelby GT500

Supercharged thunder, aerodynamic aggression, and the unmistakable growl that keeps me chasing perfect lap times.

Back to overview

Why it fires me up

The Shelby GT500 is a testament to relentless engineering. Its hand-built supercharged V8 delivers instant torque and a soundtrack that echoes through any paddock. I study its cooling ducts, aero work, and composite components to inspire my own university design projects.

Quick specs

Power
760 hp @ 7,300 rpm
0-60 mph
3.3 seconds
Top speed
180 mph
Engine
5.2L supercharged V8

Track notes

I'm captivated by how Ford Performance fine-tuned the GT500's MagneRide suspension and dual-clutch transmission. The way it balances brute force with poise makes it a perfect case study for my coursework on vehicle dynamics and control systems.

Its cabin strikes a balance between everyday driveability and race-ready focus, which fuels my interest in ergonomic design. Every dial, paddle, and control is purposefully placed for quick reactions on the circuit.

Project inspiration

  • Studying air-flow management for my aerodynamics module
  • Applying composite material learnings to student builds
  • Documenting sound design for multimedia photo edits
